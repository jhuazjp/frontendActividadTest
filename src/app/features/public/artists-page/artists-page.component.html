FOR TATTOO ARTIST

Hello, artist! This blog is specially created for the new generation of tattoo artists, those who are asking:

  • What do I need to become a tattoo artist?
  • Where can I find a mentor or a tattoo school?
  • How can I make a living from tattooing?

With over a decade of experience learning and working in the art of tattooing, these articles are the response to the questions and doubts that emerge at the beginning and as I progress in my journey as a tattoo artist.

From fundamental techniques to how to travel the world while tattooing, I'm here to show you how I've done it-and how I'm still doing it. I'll share tips, recommendations, and experiences that have helped me grow in my career.

This blog provides the inspiration and support you need to create your own guide, whether you're a beginner or refining your skills as a tattoo artist.

Let's create art together!
